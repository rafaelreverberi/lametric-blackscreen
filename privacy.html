BlackScreen for LaMetric Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-08-31

Who we are

BlackScreen is a minimal LaMetric app that displays a completely black screen. It is designed for users who want zero distractions and a dimmed display.

What data we process

  • The app does not collect, store, or process any personal data.
  • No cookies, trackers, analytics, or profiling are used.
  • The app fetches a static JSON file hosted on GitHub Pages which defines an empty frame. This file contains no personal information.

Purpose of processing

The sole purpose of the app is to show a black screen on the LaMetric device.
